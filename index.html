how do i use clavaa pay?

Simply sign up in the app or website and scan the Clavaa QR code at stores accepting us.

How secure is Clavaa?

Clavaa is a secure and easy-to-use mobile payment method that allows you to pay at stores and online by simply scanning a QR code. To get started, sign up for Clavaa through the app or website, and link your bank account using Plaid. With Clavaa, your bank account number and identity are not shared with merchants and your bank account numbers aren’t stored on your device or on our servers, making it safer than using a physical credit, debit, or prepaid card. Additionally, when you pay in stores, neither Clavaa nor your device will send your actual card number to merchants, and when you pay online, the merchant will only receive the information necessary to fulfill your order, such as your name, email address, and billing and shipping addresses.

Which Banks allow Clavaa?

Clavaa works with almost every single bank that has a connection to Plaid, and there is no extra cost to use it. If you don't see your bank listed, please contact us for assistance.

How much cashback can I earn?

The cashback you earn by using Clavaa will vary depending on each merchant, with a minimum of 2% and a maximum of 10%. We strive to maximize the amount our customers earn.

Can I use credit cards for payments

Currently, Clavaa only accepts bank account for payment, but we are planning to implement a credit card feature in the future.

When will Clavaa be available across the US

We are working hard to rollout Clavaa for very bussiness in the US. Keep on the lookout for when we launch across the US
